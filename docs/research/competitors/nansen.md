# Competitor: Nansen
- **URL:** https://www.nansen.ai/
- **Category:** direct
- **Core Value Prop:** On-chain analytics with wallet labeling and smart money tracking
- **Tech Stack:** React frontend, custom EVM indexers, PostgreSQL, ClickHouse for analytics
- **Key Features:**
  1. Wallet labeling (250M+ addresses labeled across 15+ chains)
  2. Smart Money dashboard (track fund/exchange/whale movements)
  3. Token God Mode (see all holders + their PnL for any token)
  4. DeFi Paradise (yield farming analytics)
  5. NFT analytics (floor tracking, whale minting patterns)
- **Known Weaknesses:** $150/month minimum, limited free tier, no macro/TradFi data, no news, no prediction markets
- **Pricing Model:** Free (limited) → $150/mo → $1,500/mo → Enterprise
- **User Traction:** 200,000+ users, $10M+ ARR
- **Last Major Update:** Multi-chain expansion to 15+ chains (2025)
- **Unique Differentiator:** Wallet labeling quality — they label more addresses with higher accuracy than anyone
- **What their users complain about:** Expensive, slow UI, limited free tier, no real-time alerts, no crypto-native news
