# Competitor: Arkham Intelligence
- **URL:** https://www.arkhamintelligence.com/
- **Category:** direct
- **Core Value Prop:** Entity-level blockchain intelligence — who is doing what on-chain
- **Tech Stack:** Custom AI-powered entity labeling, React frontend, multi-chain indexers
- **Key Features:**
  1. Entity labels (exchanges, funds, whales, protocols — 500K+ entities)
  2. Visual entity graph (knowledge graph of wallet relationships)
  3. Real-time entity tracking (alert when labeled entity moves funds)
  4. Cross-chain wallet profiling
  5. On-chain forensics (trace fund flows between entities)
- **Known Weaknesses:** Free tier is very limited, no macro/TradFi, no DeFi analytics, no news
- **Pricing Model:** Free (limited) → $99/mo → Enterprise
- **User Traction:** 500,000+ users, dominant in on-chain forensics
- **Last Major Update:** Multi-chain entity graph (2025)
- **Unique Differentiator:** AI-powered entity labeling — they identify who owns which wallets
- **What their users complain about:** Slow graph rendering, limited free queries, no real-time alerts in free tier
